About Me

With a deep-rooted passion for Hindustani classical music, I specialize in Dhrupad and voice culture, known for its profound depth and rich vocal tradition. Trained in the Dagarvani style, I have pursued formal education from Visva Bharati Santiniketan (B.A. & M.A.) and am currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Indian classical music from Barkatullah University, Bhopal. My musical journey includes guidance under esteemed gurus like Shri Murli Dhar Jagudi, Ma Aloka Nandy, and Shri Niloy Ahsan, along with time spent at Ustad Nasir Moinuddin Dhrupad Sangeet Ashram and with the Gundecha Brothers.
I offer individual and group classes in Hindustani Classical Vocal, with a focus on developing voice, understanding Raag, and mastering rhythm through Dhrupad and Dhamaar. With over 7.5 years of teaching experience, both online and offline, my goal is to help each student connect deeply with sound and tradition.

What We Do

🎶 Learn Hindustani Classical Vocal Music

Discover the rich tradition of Hindustani Classical Music through personalized vocal training designed to suit your learning pace and style. Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to deepen your existing practice, there's a class format for everyone.

🌱Foundational Training

Our beginner-level training is thoughtfully designed to build a strong musicalfoundation while keeping the learning journey engaging and enjoyable.

🎵What You’ll Learn:
  • Understanding Notes (Swar/Swar Sthan):
    • Learn the placement and significance of notes in the context of the Raag you’re studying.
  • Introduction to Raag & Bandish:
    • Begin exploring a Raag followed by a simple Bandish (song) to grasp its essence in a melodic and enjoyable way.
  • Basic Sargam Patterns (Alankaar/Palta):
    • Practice foundational sargam patterns to strengthen your vocal control and precision.
  • Elementary Rhythm & Taal Training:
    • Get familiar with basic rhythmic concepts, practice sargam with rhythm, and explore simple Taals for better musical timing.

    📅Class Frequency Recommendation:
    To ensure steady progress, students at the beginner level are encouraged to take at least two sessions per week. While three sessions are ideal, a minimum of two will help maintain consistency and retention.


    🎶 Intermediate Level

    Designed for learners who already have a basic foundation in Hindustani classical music, the intermediate course takes your skills to the next level.

    🎵What You’ll Learn:
  • Refining Raags Already Known:
    • Build on the raags you've previously learned with deeper exploration and enhanced application of Taal and Sargam techniques.
  • Personalized Pathways:
    • Progress is based on your previous experience and practice. Some students may require a few months to brush up past learning before moving ahead.

    📅 Class Frequency Recommendation:
    Intermediate students have the flexibility to choose one or two sessions per week, based on their comfort and goals.


    🎶Advanced Level

    This level is ideal for professional or well-trained vocalists looking to refine their technique, enhance their expression, or receive guidance on intricate aspects of their musical style.

    🎵 What You’ll Focus On:
  • Tailored Vocal Coaching:
    • Get expert support to elevate your performance style, vocal control, and presentation.

    📅Class Frequency Recommendation:
    Advanced students are recommended to take one session per week for focused, high-quality mentorship


    Special Sessions

    Occasionally, special classes will be offered focusing on a specific Raag, Bandish, or Taal. These sessions are open to students across all levels—basic, intermediate, or advanced—depending on group interest and availability.

    📅 How It Works:
  • You'll be notified 7–10 days in advance about upcoming special sessions.
  • These sessions are free of additional charge and provide a wonderful chance to explore something new or revisit a concept with fresh perspective.
